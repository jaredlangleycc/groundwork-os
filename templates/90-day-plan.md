## Weekly ritual

Every Monday morning, 30 minutes:

1. Run `/weekly-brief`
2. Check progress against the 30-day milestone
3. Tag this week's activities with revenue-mapper
4. Confirm protected time is intact
5. Pick the ONE thing that matters most this week

## Quarter close (day 90)

When day 90 hits, run `/decision-logger` to capture:

- Hit / miss / mixed?
- What worked?
- What didn't?
- What gets cut for next quarter?
- What gets doubled down on?

Then re-run `/customize-from-assessment` if your stage shifted (e.g., pre-rev → early operator), and write the next 90-day plan.
