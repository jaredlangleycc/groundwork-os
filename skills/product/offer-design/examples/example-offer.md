v1.0
Locked: 2026-04-28
Source: offer-design 6-part build, validated against 5 closed engagements

## The pitch (1 sentence)

A 90-day coaching engagement that helps frontline sales managers in their first 18 months at B2B SaaS companies build the flagship offer framework (Presence, Performance, Wholeness) so they can hit team quota and own number, with a system they can run after the engagement ends.

---

## What you get (deliverable)

- 12 weekly 60-minute 1:1 coaching calls over 90 days (recorded, Zoom)
- A shared Foundation document built collaboratively over the 12 weeks
- Async email and text support, response within 24 business hours
- Pre-session reflection form before each call (online)
- 30-day post-engagement check-in call (week 16)
- Access to the the example coaching business lead-gen assessment assessment
- Coaching session reference card (battle card) for daily use during the engagement

## Guarantee

**30-day money-back guarantee.**

If at any point in the first 30 days the engagement is not delivering value, the client requests a refund and receives the full $X,XXX back. No questions asked. No refund-clawback for the calls already delivered.

After day 30, the engagement is committed for the full 90 days. No partial refunds for the remaining 60 days. If the client cannot continue for any reason, the engagement pauses and resumes within 6 months at no additional cost (one pause per engagement maximum).

If the coach (Jared) misses more than one weekly call without 48 hours notice, the client may request a full refund of unused weeks, no questions asked.

## This is not for you if

- You are looking for a thinking partner without a specific sales management role to improve in (Foundation is built around live role execution)
- You are a VP, Director, or C-suite leader with 5+ years of leadership experience (Foundation is built for first-time managers in their first 18 months)
- You want unlimited 24/7 access or evening/weekend sessions (the Family Floor is non-negotiable; engagement runs business hours only)
- You are unwilling to do the pre-session reflection work between calls (the reflection is the engine of the engagement)
- You are looking for a passive course or framework you can consume on your own time (this is a practice, not a curriculum)
- You are in active job-search mode or considering leaving your current role (career transition coaching is a separate domain)
- You need group / team coaching for your reports (separate engagement)
- You expect outcome guarantees beyond the 30-day money-back (the coaching outcome depends on your engagement in the work, which the coach cannot guarantee)

## Operational notes (internal, not customer-facing)

### Capacity math

- 90 days × 1 hour/wk per client = 12-13 hours delivery per engagement
- Plus 30-min pre-call prep + 30-min post-call notes = 1 hour overhead per call
- Total per engagement: ~24-26 hours of coach time over 90 days
- Max concurrent engagements at 12-15 hrs/wk in business: 4-5 active engagements
- 4 active × $X,XXX / 90 days = ~$10K/mo MRR equivalent
- Margin: very high (no software, minimal overhead)

### Refund history (as of 2026-05-23)

- 0 refunds in 5 closed engagements (Sam, Jordan, Riley, Avery-OS-adjacent, Drew pilot)
- 0 mid-engagement pauses
- 1 client (Jordan) elected Ongoing Coaching post-engagement

### Common scope-creep requests (handled at offer-design time)

- "Can my boss join one call?": No. Engagement is 1:1, confidential. (Boss can have a separate engagement.)
- "Can you help me prep for my performance review?": Yes, that is within scope of role execution. The review prep IS the coaching work.
- "Can you write me a recommendation letter at the end?": No. Out of scope, and a conflict-of-interest pattern.
- "Can we extend by another 3 months?": Yes, via the Ongoing Coaching offer. Not the same as extending Foundation.
- "Can I gift this to a colleague?": No. Engagement is non-transferable. Refer the colleague to book their own discovery call.

### Pricing rationale (referenced from pricing-strategy)

- $X,XXX anchors against executive coaching ($25-50K) at ~5-10% the price
- Anchors against self-paced courses ($300-1,500) at 2-8x the price
- $X,XXX fits the buying authority of a Sales Manager personal credit card (under typical $3K personal-spend threshold)
- $X,XXX fits 90-day buying frame (1 quarter of forecasting)
- 30-day money-back has produced 0 refunds in 5 engagements (low refund risk, high close lift)
