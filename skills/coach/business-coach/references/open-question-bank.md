# Open Question Bank

50+ open questions organized by situation. Use these as starters, adapt the wording to the user's coach archetype and the specific context.

## Decision-making

- What's the actual decision in front of you right now?
- What would change if you decided this tomorrow versus next month?
- Who else does this decision affect?
- What does a 6-month-from-now version of you want this to be?
- What would you tell a client doing exactly this?
- Where are you bullshitting yourself about this?
- What's the thing you keep avoiding bringing up?
- If you couldn't change your mind for 90 days, which would you pick?
- What's the smallest version of this you could commit to?
- What would you lose if you said no to all of this?

## Stuck / paralyzed

- What's the thing you've been meaning to do for 3+ weeks?
- What changed recently that you haven't named yet?
- What's the part you're embarrassed about?
- If you had to drop two things from your week, which two?
- What would your last week look like if you trusted yourself?
- What's the question you wish I'd ask?
- Where's the gap between what you say you want and what you're actually doing?
- What are you protecting by staying stuck here?
- If you only had 90 minutes today, what would you spend them on?

## Sales / pipeline

- Who's the last prospect you ghosted instead of closing?
- What's the deal you'd take that you shouldn't?
- What's the deal you wouldn't take that you should?
- If your top prospect said no tomorrow, what'd change about your week?
- What's the offer your best clients keep asking for that you haven't built?
- Where's the friction your prospects hit and you keep meaning to fix?

## Pricing

- What would change if you raised prices 30% tomorrow?
- Who's paying you the least that you'd be relieved to lose?
- What's the price you'd refuse below, even if you needed the money?
- If you charged double, who'd actually pay?
- What's the price you'd quote a stranger versus your network?

## Content / marketing

- What's the thing you're avoiding writing about because it's too honest?
- What did you tell a client this week that should be a post?
- What post would you write if no one in your network was watching?
- What's a topic you have an actually contrarian take on?
- Where's the gap between what you publish and what you'd say at a dinner?

## Brand / voice

- What do you sound like when you're really yourself?
- What's a phrase you say constantly that should be in your brand?
- What do you refuse to do that competitors do?
- Who are you the antidote to?
- What would your three best clients say is the reason they hired you?

## Burnout / floor

- What protected time did you break this week?
- What did you say yes to that you should have refused?
- Who's getting the version of you they shouldn't be (work over family, or vice versa)?
- What's the floor you'd never let yourself go below?
- What does an actually-good week look like right now?

## Strategy / next quarter

- What's working that you could double?
- What's not working that you keep doing?
- What did you commit to 90 days ago that didn't happen?
- What would you build if money wasn't a question this quarter?
- What would you sunset if no one would notice?
- What's the bet you'd make if you trusted yourself?

## Money

- What's the burn number you'd panic at?
- What's the revenue number you'd celebrate at?
- What's the gap between those two and what's the time horizon?
- What would change if you had 18 months of runway instead of 6?
- What would change if you had 3 months instead of 12?

## Risk / expansion

- What's the worst case if this works?
- What's the worst case if this doesn't?
- What's the regret you'd carry from not doing it?
- Who in your life would you have to consult before saying yes?
- What can you reverse later if you start now?

## Coach-mode meta

- What's the question you wish I asked last time?
- What's the conversation you're avoiding having?
- Where am I cheerleading you when I should be challenging?
- Where am I challenging you when you needed steady?
- What would you want me to push you on for the next 30 days?

## Usage notes

- Pick 1-2 from the appropriate bucket. Don't barrage.
- Adapt wording to match the user's archetype (Challenger gets sharper phrasing, Reflective gets longer-stem phrasing).
- If a question lands hard and the user gets quiet, leave space. Don't rescue with a softer question.
- Recycle good questions across conversations. They work because they re-orient.
