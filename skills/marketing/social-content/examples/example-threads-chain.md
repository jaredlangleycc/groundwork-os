## What works in this chain

- **First line earns the click.** "Most new sales managers think the problem is time management" is a complete claim. Reader knows what they're getting.
- **No "X, not Y" antipattern.** Post 5 land line uses "isn't" once but the second sentence states affirmatively what the fix IS (the pause).
- **Specific details.** 23 minutes, 4:45 pm, "three to five seconds." Specificity beats abstraction.
- **One trigger per post.** Posts 2/3/4 don't blend. Reader can save or quote any individual post.
- **The asset link is post 5, not post 1.** Reader earned the link by reading the chain.

## What this chain does NOT do

- No emojis (Langley brand convention).
- No hashtags. Threads search is light; hashtags don't earn reach.
- No engagement bait ("repost if you've been there").
- No "follow for more" closer. The reader already followed (or didn't).

## Engagement signal (first 4 hours)

Track these. If under threshold, diagnose:

| Signal | Threshold | Diagnosis if under |
|---|---|---|
| Replies on post 1 | 3+ in first hour | Hook is off or post-time wrong |
| Replies on post 5 | 5+ in 4 hours | The land + assignment didn't land |
| Link clicks (essay) | 2% of impressions | The asset bridge is wrong |
| Reposts of post 1 | 1+ in 4 hours | Hook is fine, body is the problem |
| Reposts of post 5 | 3+ in 4 hours | Body landed, land line resonated |

If post 5 outperforms post 1, the chain worked. If post 1 outperforms post 5, the chain's hook earned attention but the body lost the reader.

## Voice notes

- "Slack", proper noun, capitalized
- "Wednesday," "Friday," "Monday", proper nouns
- "Three to five seconds", written out, not "3-5 seconds" (matches Langley voice for ambient cadence)
- No em-dashes anywhere
- No "just" or "really" anywhere
- "set the agenda", Langley signature phrase, used naturally
- "see what shows up", also Langley signature phrase
