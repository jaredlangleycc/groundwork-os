# Topic Cluster: Presence Pillar (Worked Example)

One pillar fully expanded into 6 topic clusters, each producing 7 derivative pieces. This is a full quarter (12 weeks) of Presence pillar content. Use as a model for how to mine one pillar deeply.

## Pillar: Presence

> Stop reactive leadership. Show up fully in every moment.

ICP problem statements that map to Presence:

- "I'm always reacting, never planning"
- "My one-on-ones are status reads, not coaching"
- "I keep getting pulled into low-stakes fires"
- "I can't slow down enough to think strategically"
- "I'm in every meeting and remember none of them"
- "My team brings me problems but never solutions"

## Cluster volume

6 clusters × 7 derivatives = 42 pieces of content from this one pillar.

At 10-12 posts per week with the spine pattern, Presence pillar alone fuels 4 weeks (one cluster per week, mining the 7 derivatives).

3 pillars × 6 clusters = 18 clusters = 12 to 18 weeks of content.

## How to use this as a template

For each pillar:

1. Brainstorm 6 to 8 core ideas the pillar contains.
2. Write each as a sentence-length claim.
3. Test each against the pillar test (does it ladder to offer? does it use ICP language?).
4. Pick 6.
5. Expand each into 7 derivatives using the spine.

A quarter of content takes 4 to 6 hours to plan once. Production then runs against the plan.

Don't try to mine 6 clusters in one sitting. Do 2 per week for 3 weeks. Sleep on each. The third pass catches the cluster's real shape.

## When to retire a cluster

Retire a cluster when:

- It hit, you got 30+ DMs, and the user already onboarded the converts → cycle to a new cluster next quarter
- It missed, 0 DMs, 0 saves, 0 inbound conversations → diagnose: was it the cluster's claim, the user's voice on it, or the audience saturation?
- It tested a pillar that didn't pass the pillar test on review → kill cluster + reconsider pillar

Don't retire a cluster after one essay. Some clusters need 3 to 6 weeks for the message to land. Patience for the spine. Speed only for the production.
