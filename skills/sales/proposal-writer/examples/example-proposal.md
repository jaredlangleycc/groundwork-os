## FAQ

**How do sessions work?**
60 min on Microsoft Teams. Standard structure: 10-min check-in on what
shifted since last session, 35-min on the core work for this week, 15-min
on what to take away. Sessions are recorded for your reference.

**What if I'm too busy for the homework between sessions?**
The engagement assumes 60-90 min per week of reflection and one
experiment. If a week gets crushed, we adjust the next session to start
where you are. Goal is specific shifts that hold up under your real
schedule, not perfect homework compliance.

**Right time to start?**
Practical answer: kickoff session within 7 days of payment received.
You mentioned Q3 board prep in October. We'll plan around that. The
work doesn't require empty calendar, it requires honest engagement.

**What if I want to convince my wife this is worth it before I sign?**
Two options:
A) I can join a 30-min call with both of you to answer her questions
directly. No cost.
B) I can send a 1-pager built for the at-home conversation, framing why
this is also a Wholeness investment for the family. You forward to her.
Option A converts higher. Option B is faster. Tell me which fits.

**What happens after the 90 days?**
Three paths:
A) Take what you've built and run with it. No further commitment. Door
open if you want to come back later.
B) Ongoing coaching at $250/mo (graduate rate), lighter cadence
(1-2 sessions/mo).
C) Another 90-day sprint at $X,XXX with a new focus.
Most clients pick A or B. The right choice depends on what you've
integrated by day 90.

**How do I cancel a session?**
24-hour notice to reschedule, no penalty. Less than 24 hours, the session
is considered held. Exception: real emergencies, no penalty regardless of notice.

## Notes on customization for Alex specifically

What was pulled from the free intro call and built into THIS proposal (vs. a generic flagship-offer proposal):

1. **Section 2 opens with Alex's actual quote** ("I keep firefighting"). This is the proof Jared listened.

2. **Section 2 names the specific pillar** (Wholeness) tied to the specific moment (Sunday 9 PM rep call review). The hypothesized pain from the call-prep brief was confirmed on the call, so it's now explicitly named in the proposal.

3. **Section 3 outcomes are specific to Alex's situation** (1:1 cadence, boundary between sales-leader and dad). NOT generic "leadership clarity" outcomes.

4. **Section 6 outcome example is Sam** (Director at Series B SaaS, similar profile to Alex). Selected because it's the closest analog Jared has. Sam's testimonial is live, name is approved for use.

5. **FAQ #4 ("convince my wife")** is included because Alex mentioned his daughter and family on the call. Wife / partner buy-in is a real objection for this prospect.

6. **FAQ #3 mentions Q3 board prep in October** specifically because Alex named it on the call. Generic FAQ would say "if you have a known scheduling block, name it." This version names it explicitly.

7. **Decision deadline is 14 days from send.** Standard. Creates urgency without manufactured pressure.

8. **Pricing is $X,XXX LOCKED** to the offer spec. No discount, no scope-adjustment. Both payment options offered.

## Voice scan results

- 0 em dashes (rewrote scope bullets that originally had them, used commas/periods)
- 0 instances of "just", "really", "basically", "actually"
- 0 "X, not Y" patterns
- 0 banned closes
- 0 AI tells
- Imperative form throughout (e.g., "Sign below and send" not "You should sign")
- Specific prospect-quote opens Section 2

Voice profile matched: Steady-Hand + Challenger blend. Tone calibrated for Alex's archetype (Engine, low Wholeness, slight overwhelm visible on calls). Slightly softer than full Drill-Sergeant calibration would produce, slightly sharper than full Reflective.

## What was deliberately NOT included

- Generic "powerful coaching framework" language
- Stock testimonials from companies not directly relevant to Alex's situation
- A "social proof" wall of logos
- "Limited time" pricing urgency
- Multiple competing tiers (would create decision paralysis)
- An "upgrade path" that pre-sells the next engagement before this one starts
- Generic FAQ items about coaching as a category (Alex already understands coaching from his research call)

The proposal is 1300 words. Tight. Reads in 8 min. Decision in 14 days.
