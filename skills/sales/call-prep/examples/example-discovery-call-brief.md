## Notes on what worked / what to learn for future briefs

This brief was used May 22. Post-call notes (added retroactively):

**What worked:**
- Q4 (Sunday question) confirmed the hypothesis in 90 seconds. Alex said
  "honestly, last Sunday I started reviewing rep calls at 9pm."
- The redirect prep on pricing came in handy. He asked about cost at the
  35-min mark. The R2.1 redirect gave us 15 more minutes of substance.
- Mirroring "firefighting" instead of using my own words landed. He
  visibly relaxed when I said "the firefighting feeling" in min 22.

**What to refine for next brief:**
- Hypothesized pain section was too long. Could be tighter, 2-3 lines max.
- Should have included one specific question about his VP, who he quoted
  positively. The "whose voice is in your head" question (Q6) covered it
  but a direct VP question would have been faster.
- Skip the "PRE-SESSION FORM NOT SUBMITTED" line in future. It's noise.
  Just plan to cover the ground live.

- Alex booked strategy call for week of June 1 before the call ended.
- Sent the 1-pager 24 hours later as bonus value.
