# Full Ladder (Extended Functions)

The 11 core functions in SKILL.md cover most solopreneur needs. This document handles the niche functions and the edge cases.

Verify free tiers and pricing at time of recommendation. SaaS pricing shifts quarterly.

## Podcast hosting

| Rung | Tool | Cost | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0 | Anchor (Spotify for Podcasters) | Free | Unlimited episodes, basic stats |
| 1 | Transistor Starter or Buzzsprout Basic | $19/mo | Better analytics, custom domain |
| 2 | Transistor Pro | $49/mo | Multi-show, advanced stats |
| 3 | Transistor Business or self-hosted | $99+/mo | Multi-show enterprise |

**Default for solopreneur:** Rung 0 until 1,000 downloads per episode. Most podcasts never need to climb.

## Edge case: when no free path exists

Some functions have no real free path for solopreneurs:

- **Domain registration.** $10-15/yr is the floor. Recommend Cloudflare Registrar (at-cost pricing).
- **Business email with custom domain.** $6/mo via Google Workspace Starter or M365 Business Basic. Cloudflare Email Routing is "free" but forwards only.
- **Stripe/Square processing fees.** No free path. The fees are the cost of accepting payment.
- **State business filings.** LLC formation costs $40-500 depending on state.

For these, the "boring stack" choice is "lowest necessary cost," not "free tier."

## Edge case: when the user is locked in

The user is on Webflow Workspace at $39/mo. They want to switch to Cloudflare Pages free. Their site has 12 months of CMS content.

The boring-stack answer is: do not switch. Migration cost (time + risk) exceeds the savings. Document the lock-in in `decisions/free-first-attempts.md` and revisit at contract renewal.

Climbs are easier than descents. The ladder is designed to make climbs honest and descents rare.

## Edge case: when free tier disappears

Calendly stripped multi-event-types from free tier in 2024. Mailchimp gated automations to paid in 2022. Notion changed AI pricing in 2023.

When a vendor strips a free tier, the user's existing setup is grandfathered for some period (varies by vendor), then forced to climb. Update `decisions/free-first-attempts.md` with the vendor change. Run `free-first-checker` to identify the cheapest alternative free path. Switching vendors at the moment of free-tier removal is often cheaper than climbing on the original.
